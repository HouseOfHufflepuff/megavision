The Fan Formula

How each team's final 25/26 fan count is built, live from the league sheet and Fantrax

Fans = Standings + Firm&Legacy + Top XI + MBP + Points Bonus
· Standings: tiered by live Fantrax rank (1st = 220 fans down to 12th = 25)
· Firm + Legacy: career/trophy legacy value, from the league sheet
· Top XI: 20 fans per player a team owns in the league's real live Top XI (1 GK / 3 D / 4 M / 3 F, ranked by actual Fantrax fantasy points)
· MBP: 50 fans for owning the single highest-scoring player in the league — currently Bruno Fernandes (REN, 340.5 pts)
· Points Bonus: fixed bonus for the top 5 teams by season points, from the league sheet
