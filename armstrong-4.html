BLACKBURN ROVERS (Loan 2017–18, Permanent 2018–2021)

21 appearances and 9 goals on loan convinced Rovers to make it permanent: £1.75 million, signed 6 August 2018. What followed was, frankly, one of the great modern Championship striker runs: 130 appearances, 49 goals. THREE hat-tricks in the 2020-21 season alone. Voted Blackburn Rovers Player of the Season for 2019-20. If you know, you know. If you don't know, you are about to know, because we are about to tell you three more times on this page.

Three hat-tricks. In one season. We are going to say it again for the people in the back: THREE HAT-TRICKS.
